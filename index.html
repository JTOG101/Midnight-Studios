About Midnight Studios

Midnight Studios blends strength, sensuality, and self-expression. Our certified instructors come from dance, aerial arts, and fitness backgrounds. We offer evening and weekend classes in a body-positive space designed for beginners and seasoned dancers alike.

Our Classes

  • Beginner Pole Dancing – Build confidence and learn foundational moves.
  • Pole Dancing Workout – Sculpt your body with dynamic, strength-based routines.
  • Advanced Pole Flow – Elevate your artistry and technique.
  • Private Sessions – Book for parties, celebrations, or team events.

Private Bookings

Celebrate with movement! Midnight Studios offers private bookings for birthdays, bachelorette parties, and team-building events. Contact us to customize your experience.
